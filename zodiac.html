Zodiac Signs

Aquarius

January 20-February 18

Aquarius sign belongs to the Air element and is ruled by Uranus. They are intelligent, humanitarian, unconventional, and noncommittal.

Pisces

February 19-March 20

Pisces sign belongs to the Water element and is ruled by Neptune. They are romantic, imaginative, illusional, and wistful.

Aries

March 21-April 19

Aries sign belongs to the Fire element and is ruled by Mars. They are adventurous, confident, self-centered, and impulsive.

Taurus

April 20-May 20

Taurus sign belongs to the Earth element and is ruled by Venus. They are strong-willed, unwavering, stubborn, down to earth, loyal.

Gemini

May 21-June 20

Gemini sign belongs to the Air sign and are ruled by Mercury. They are communicative, changeable, inconsistent, and superficial.

Cancer

June 21- July 22

Cancer sign belongs to the Water element and are ruled by the moon. They are devoted, compassionate, emotional, moody, and hesitant.

Leo

July 23-August 22

Leo sign belongs to the Fire element and ruled by the Sun. They are loyal strong, consistent, and possessive.

Virgo

August 23-September 22

Virgo sign belongs to the earth element and is ruled by Mercury. They are dependable, patient, critical, and judgmental.

Libra

Sep 23-Oct 22

Libra sign belongs to the air element and is ruled by Venus. They are charming, loving, indecisive, and lazy.

Scorpio

October 23-November 21

Scorpio sign belongs to the water element and is ruled by both Pluto and Mars.They are passionate, charismatic, secretive, and manipulative.

Sagittarius

November 22-December 21

Sagittarius sign belongs to the Fire element and is ruled by Jupiter. They are gregarious, sociable, blunt, and restless.

Capricorn

December 22-January 19

Capricorn sign belongs to the Earth element and is ruled by Saturn. They are willful, serious, rigid, and demanding.
