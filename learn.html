KEEP OUR SKY BEAUTIFUL

Air pollution is a global issue and without your help, beautiful night skies like this will be covered by smog and pollution. In addition to losing of our view of our skies, air pollution is also very dangerous. According to the World Health Organization, over 4.2 million people die each year from exposure to air pollution. Additionally, In the U.S. nearly 134 million people are at risk of disease and premature death because of air pollution, according to American Lung Association estimates.
